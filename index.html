This is a playable keyboard in Just Intonation, with keys positioned according to their harmonic relationships.
It's based on this video visualizing the harmony of a song, and on the blog posts explaining it.
You can click the notes or use your qwerty keyboard. Hold shift or space when starting a note to move it up or down one octave.
Moving east represents going up a fifth. Moving northeast represents going up a major third. All the notes are shifted to be the same octave as the tonic.
(You get the blue notes by multiplying the pitch by 7. They're not in the standard western scale.)
