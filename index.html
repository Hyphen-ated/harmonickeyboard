Harmonic Lattice Piano

Use mouse and/or keyboard. Your keyboard probably can't press many buttons at once.
Press shift or space while holding click to adjust the octave of a button up or down.
The tonic is hz. Or set it by name:

About

This is a keyboard in Just Intonation, with keys positioned according to their harmonic relationships.
It's based on this video visualizing the harmony of a song, and on the blog posts explaining it.
Moving right represents going up by a fifth. Moving up-right represents going up by a major third. All the notes are shifted to be the same octave as the tonic.
